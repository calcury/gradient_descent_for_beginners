Learning Path Explanation

This series of course materials is based on real automotive fuel consumption data. We start with the simplest single variable, identify the bottlenecks of mathematical formula methods, then introduce loss functions and gradient descent, and ultimately solve complex prediction problems with high-dimensional multiple features.
